About me

I am Pulin Pramod Prabhu, an aspiring product professional specializing in Mobile, Web, and Data products. With a Master's degree in Management Information Systems from Texas A&M University and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Mumbai, I possess a strong educational background that has laid the foundation for my success.

Throughout my career, I have been driven by results and have leveraged data-driven insights to enhance user experience and drive product success. By employing agile methodologies and customer-centric approaches, I have consistently delivered high-quality products that meet and exceed user expectations.

Critical thinking, problem-solving, leadership, and entrepreneurship have been instrumental in my success and so have been recognizing the importance of agility in today's business environment. I played a crucial role in driving the adoption of agile practices across global teams. This initiative significantly improved delivery efficiency and fostered a more collaborative and adaptable work culture.
